In section 1.1 of w3.org, the main topic of discussion is different ways to
present information for a non text situation.

Within section 1.2 the focus is time based media. This can be applied to
pre recorded audio and video, it can be used for captions that synchronize with

in different way without losing data or is structure.

Finally in section 1.4 the topic is making creating information that stands
out from other content and the background. A few ways someone can do
this using audio, use different text sizes, and maybe even using contrast.
